class datetime.cores.DateTimeIntervalCore

Available on all platforms

Time interval representation. Stores difference in seconds between two DateTime instances. Amounts of years/months/days/hours/minutes/seconds are calculated accounting leap years. * Maximum allowed interval is ~4100 years.
